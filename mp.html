Logo Design Iterative Process

  • Conceptualization:

    Firstly , I thought hard about what I wanted to do and I decided I wanted to incorporate the first letter of my surname and first name.

  • Color Exploration:

    I then thought abut what colors I wanted to use , I chose an orange-like hue for the crown atop the 'I' symbolizing aspirations for excellence.

  • Typeface Selection:

    I Opted for the Algerian font for the text in the logo, and I chose it for its vintage style adding sophistication and timelessness.

  • Iterations:

    • Initial Concepts: I began with rough sketches exploring different 'IP'arrangements and styles , and after about 10 sketches ,I decided to incorporate a crown to replace the dot atop the 'I'

    • Feedback Loop: I decided to seek feedback from my peers, and I refined the concept based on their insights, leading to a more polished design.

    • Color Trials: I then Experimented with various color schemes and ended up with the orange crown for vibrancy and significance, but for the letters I wanted to stick with black because every other color i tried looked weird

  • Refinement:

    Typography Tweaks: I tried different fonts to before i was finally able to achieve the perfect balance between 'IP' and the chosen font.

  • Finalization:

    Consistency Check: I Ensured visual consistency across different applications and sizes.
