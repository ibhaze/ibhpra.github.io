TRIPLE-S

In the process of group formation, we named ourselves TRIPLE-s, signifying our third semester and focus on strategic research, swift development, and stylish designs. We began by drafting a contract agreement to establish our rules, demonstrating adherence to professional standards (LO4). Engaging in collaborative brainstorming sessions, we collectively defined our brand identity, with each member contributing logo iterations. My contribution involved thorough research on logos incorporating the letter "s," showcasing my ability to conceptualize and develop interactive media products (LO1). Iteratively refining our designs based on feedback, we adopted a logo crafted by Danya, demonstrating our commitment to creative iterations (LO3). Feedback from peers favored a fan-like logo over alternatives, leading us to refine and finalize our design, displaying personal leadership (LO5) in aligning our branding choices with stakeholder preferences and ethical considerations.
