PROJECT

Our project for the semester involved creating a personalized nutrition plan for users utilizing generative AI. Our client, Livewall, is deeply immersed in the world of generative AI, presenting an excellent opportunity for me to enhance my skills in this domain. Using the double diamond method We initiated the project by conducting primary research on user preferences in nutrition apps or websites and formulated a survey based on the findings. Our research indicated that users primarily seek nutrition plans to maintain their health or achieve weight loss goals. Subsequently, I collaborated with Danya to refine the survey and targeted it towards our client's demographic, which comprises middle-aged individuals. Following this, we solicited feedback from our teacher, who suggested improvements to the survey. These included incorporating an introductory section to elucidate the concept of a nutrition plan for users and integrating a question addressing their personal preferences, such as weight loss or muscle gain. .These are the results .After multiple iterations of the survey , we created a concept document to serve as a guide for the primary and secondary research , and I was again in charge of creating the POV /HMV to kind of serve as a way of viewing the product and users from a different perspective .After this , I created interview questions alongside danya to have more quality and useful insights , We refined the interview questions after asking for feedback and from the feedback we reflected and decided to split the questions into two sections ..You can find the interview questions and transcript .I also created the low fidelity prototype for the nutrition plan on my own so that we'd have a diffferent variation of the one we created together .
